SAPA - CAT SAND - HANG THONG - FANSIPAN

DAY 01: HANOI - SAPA - HAM RONG (Lunch, dinner)

06h30 - 07h00: Pick up guests at the hotel in the Old Quarter area, depart for Sapa on Noi Bai - Lao Cai highway.

12h30: Arrive in Sapa town, local guides welcome you and take you to the hotel for lunch and then check in.

14h00: Visit Ham Rong Mountain Park, visit Indochinese orchid garden with all kinds of colorful flowers, visit Heaven Gate, Cloud Garden - where the sky and sky meet, panoramic view of Sapa and Fansipan mountain great.

19h00: After dinner, you free roam tour Sapa town, sip coffee or enjoy the BBQ, ... Overnight at the central hotel

DAY 02: SAPA - CAT CAT (Breakfast, lunch, dinner)

07h00: After breakfast, take a walk to discover Cat Cat and Thac Thuy Hydropower Park, enjoy performances and learn about culture and customs of ethnic minorities here. .

11h00: Return to the hotel for lunch and rest

Afternoon: Free to visit shopping or take photo at Sapa Stone Church.

19h00: After dinner, you are free to roam the town or attend the Sapa Love Market on Saturday evening - a traditional culture of ethnic H'mong, Dao in the highlands. Northwest. Overnight at the hotel

DAY 03: SAPA - FANSIPAN - HA NOI (Breakfast, Lunch)

Morning: After breakfast, car pick you up at the station to the cable car station, where you can buy cable car to visit the scene of the first time - Fansipan peak at an altitude of 3,143m Indochina roof ( The cost of cable car tickets and guides is not included in the terminal.)

11h00: Pick up you return to the hotel to check out, luggage at the reception and have lunch

Afternoon: Free to visit souvenir shopping at Sapa central market with many brocade and specialty items of the Northwest mountains and forests.

15h00: Goodbye Sapa, you board the car back to Hanoi. On the way stop resting

21h00 - 21h30: Go to the original destination, farewell you finish the program.
