BAI DINH - TRANG AN - CUC PHUONG 2 DAYS 1 NIGHT

DAY 1: Bai Đinh - Trang An ( L/ D )

08:00- 08:30: Tour guide picks you up at suggested places around Ha Noi Old Quarter

11:00 Arrive in Buddha Sanctuary place of BaiDinh Pagoda, the biggest one in Vietnam

13:00 Have lunch in a local restaurant with local special dishes including goat meat

14:00 Leave for Trang An eco - tourism attraction. You will be amazed by neglected area while having a boat trip through cave chains: Sang cave, Toi cave, Ba Giot cave.....experiencing mysterious views of the so - called ( Ha Long Bay in Land )

16:30 Check in hotel or Bungalow. Have free biking discovery Tam Coc Village

19:00 Have diner and relax

Day 2: Tam Coc - Cuc Phuong - Ha Noi (B / L)

07:00 Enjoy Breakfast

07:30 Depart to Cuc Phuong National Park, photographing spectacular views through county side road

09:30 Visit endangered Primate rescue Center which is home to species like golden- headed langur, Tonkin snub - Nosed Langur and Black crested gibbon.......

10:30 Experience thrilling moment when exploring cave of pre-historic man dated back 7000 years old

11:30 Enjoy a local style Vietnamese lunch at the National Park

12:30 Spend hour trekking through jungle, you winter a giant tree of thousands yours old named Cho Chi.

14:30 Get a bus transfer back to Tam Coc and join the other group back to Ha Noi

19:00 Arrival in Ha Noi. Tour ends.
